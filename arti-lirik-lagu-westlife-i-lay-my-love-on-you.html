5. Tenses yang terdapat dalam lirik lagu hate you i love you


Answer:

The song entitled 'Hate you I love you' sung by Olivia O'Brien (Gnash) mainly tells about the feeling the girl has after her boyfriend left her all alone. The entire song basically describes how she feels about him that she's still in love with him despite the fact that he forgets about her and has seen someone else.
Therefore the tenses used are mostly in present tense. Only one sentence is in present continous tense 'and all this time is passing by' and some in past tense to show what she remembered with him back then. Here are those sentences:
'Wedding bells were just alarms.'
'you said you wouldn't and you f***ing did.'

Kelas: SMP
Mapel: English
Kategori: song language analysis

Present Continuous Tense

Terdapat pada kalimat "Im holding on forever", Yang berarti bahwa si penyanyi sedang bernyanyi di masa kini dan sedang berlangsung. Rumus: S + am/is/are + Ving.

32. apa fungsi sosial, theme, verse dan chorus lagu westlife - my love ?


Answer: 

The song 'My Love' by Westlife has rather deeper meaning than just romance. If you look carefully at the lyrics, therein lies a profound or wider meaning of love here. 
The social function of the song is to express your longing (or in this case the band's longing) to their homeland, Ireland, where their family lives. The love in this song is the love for their origin, place of birth or family back home. 
The theme of the song is the person's longing for their country of origin or home (can be homeland), where they have left for a while. 
The verse of the song tells how one feels emptiness inside and remembers the days when they were still with the one they left behind at home. Such can be concluded from the phrase 'a hole inside my heart', 'reaching for the love that seems so far', 'the days we had, the songs we sang together' . 
The chorus of the song indicates that one hopes and pray that one can be together again with their family after they have been away from home for long, as it is said 'hope my dreams will take me there, where the skies are blue to see you once again.'
