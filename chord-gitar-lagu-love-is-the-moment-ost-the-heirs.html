Pembahasan

Chord atau nama purenya adalah akor yaitu sekumpulan nada-nada yang digabungkan secara bersama sehingga menimbulkan bunyi yang harmonis. Dalam alat musik gitar, ada beberapa kunci dasar dalam akord, diantaranya :

Kunci C, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 2 senar (1st) , 4 senar (2rd), dan 5 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci D, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 1 senar (2rd), dan 2 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci E, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 5 (2rd), 4 (3th).

Ada juga kunci-kunci lain biasa digunakan yaitu :

Kunci F.Kunci G.Kunci A.Kunci Dm <D minor>.Kunci Em <E minor>.Kunci Am <A minor>.

10. makna dan pesan dari lagu this is me(ost the greatest showman)pake bhs inggris y jawabnnya​


Jawaban:

The lyrics of this song tell the struggle of God's creatures who dare to appear to the outside world even though God created them 'slightly' different from other humans.

In this film, there are many characters told by humans - people who are underestimated because the 'shape' is different from other humans. They are ostracized, ridiculed, reviled, discriminated against because their form is different from other human beings and most are considered scary. In the lyrics of This Is Me, they rise from scolding and dare to show that they are the same as other humans.

Readers, all humans are the same, all humans deserve to live well, deserve to feel safe. The rights of every human being are the same, wanting to live with a sense of security and protection. Humans are not distinguished from their physical form, but from their nature and actions towards other humans or their morals. So, don't be afraid to show yourself, difference is art. Don't want to be ostracized!

13. The ost office is located ... the bus station and the police station


Jawaban:

"between" The ost office is located between the bus station and the police station.

Penjelasan:

This clause functions as an adverb of place, indicating where the post office is located.

The post office is located between the bus station and the police station.

Kalimat tersebut berarti "Kantor pos terletak di antara stasiun bus dan kantor polisi". "Between" merupakan kata keterangan tempat yang artinya "di antara". Sedangkan "the post office" berarti "kantor pos", "the bus station" berarti "stasiun bus", dan "the police station" berarti "kantor polisi".
